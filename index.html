About Finpulz

Finpulz is your daily companion in the fast-paced world of finance, business, and startups. Whether you’re a curious student, a working professional, or just someone interested in understanding the economy better — we’re here to make financial news and concepts simple, relevant, and engaging. Our platform brings you handpicked updates from Indian and global markets, IPOs, funding rounds, policy changes, and much more — all in a concise, reader-friendly format. Apart from daily news, we offer powerful tools to help you track your finances, educational explainers to break down complex topics, and a growing collection of resources to guide your learning journey. At Finpulz, we believe financial awareness should be accessible to everyone — not just market experts. That’s why we’re building a platform that informs, empowers, and evolves with our audience.
